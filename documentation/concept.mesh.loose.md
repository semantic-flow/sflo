- [[concept.identifier]]s without corresponding [[mesh-resource]]
- [[mesh-resource.node-component.flow-snapshot.working]] that differ from [[mesh-resource.node-component.flow-snapshot.default]]
- missing [[facet.system]] [[files|file]] or [[folder]]
