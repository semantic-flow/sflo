- the name of [[mesh-resource.component.slice.version]] folders, and therefore part of their IRIs.
- formats: 
  - `_vN` e.g., `v74` (useTimestampe:false)
  - `YYYY-MM-DD_HHMM_SS_vN` e.g., `2025-11-24_0142_07_v1` (useTimstamp:true)
- combines the [[concept.weave-label]] (`YYYY-MM-DD_HHMM_SS`) with the sequence number from [[concept.flow-slice]] (`_vN`)
