- the name of [[mesh-resource.component.slice.version]] folders, and therefore part of their IRIs.
- format: `YYYY-MM-DD_HHMM_SS_vN` (e.g., `2025-11-24_0142_07_v1`)
- combines the [[concept.weave-label]] (`YYYY-MM-DD_HHMM_SS`) with the sequence number from [[concept.flow-version]] (`_vN`)
