## Overview

In the Semantic Flow framework, semantic meshes are collections of namespaced units of meaning called "mesh knops." mesh knops are containers that hold flows and extend the namespace. [[Node components|mesh-resource.component]] are terminal resources that define or support a knop’s structure. The IRI itself is the sign, while the knop is the dereferenceable resource that embodies, describes, and organizes that sign.
